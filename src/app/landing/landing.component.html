About Drobee

We are a technology startup working in the field of artificial intelligence and drones for the agriculture sector. We enable governments, agribusinesses, and private farms to develop smart, cost-effective solutions that deliver efficiency, safety, and productivity. We are proud to be a Tunisian startup with 100% Tunisian staff and management. We are also proud to be the first company certified by the CAA as commercial drone pilots, and we are the first company to conduct aerial pollination of palm trees.
